about me

Initially daunted by the large time gap in my CV, I realise now that programming is like riding a bicycle: languages have evolved and new technologies have risen, but the problem-solving skills I developed studying for my degree at Edinburgh University are still there. I realise that the best way to learn in on-the-job and know that, given the opportunity, I will be able to ramp up my skills swiftly and become a productive member of the team at your company.
